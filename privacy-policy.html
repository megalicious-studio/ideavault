Privacy Policy for IdeaVault – Future Ideas

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IdeaVault ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our iOS application IdeaVault – Future Ideas ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use it.

All content you create within the App — including ideas, titles, descriptions, tags, and feature breakdowns — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers, no database, and no access to any content you create. If you delete the App, the app data may be linked to your iCloud account.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, share, or transfer your data to any outside party. There is nothing to share — we never receive your data in the first place.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external services. No third party has access to your data.

5. Exports

The App allows you to export your ideas as JSON or Markdown files. These exports are initiated solely by you and saved to your device or shared through apps of your choosing. We have no involvement in or visibility into any exports you make.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone. It is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.
